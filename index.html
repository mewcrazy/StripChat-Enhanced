A Superpowered HTML Reset

With just the right amount of everything, Pico is a great starting point for a clean and lightweight design system.

Chat Translations

Translates public and private chat messages, as well as tip goals on-the-fly in over different 100 languages with just 1 click.

True Fullscreen

Open streams in a true fullscreen which is not limited to your browser's width.

A Cleaner Chat

Hide chat users, or all users (Do Not Disturb), as well as distracting chat notices like tip goal, interactive toy, welcome bot & fan club discount messages.

More Emojis

Use regular emojis in chat rooms and private messages without the need to buy StripChat ULTIMATE.

Less Tracking

Disable the Watch History and disabled various Tracking Scripts, Tracking Cookies

Slimmer StripChat

A cleaner sidebar with less links, as well as less banners.

Many more Features

Sort Tip Menus by Token Prices, Add favorites from any cam listing, A Resizable video frame and Remove Blur in Group, Privte & Ticket Shows
